# Chick Fil A Sauce

Prep time:  10min

Nutritional values (1 tbsp):    Calories: 43kcal (2%) Carbohydrates: 7g (2%) Polyunsaturated Fat: 1g Monounsaturated Fat: 1g Cholesterol: 3mg (1%) Sodium: 124mg (5%) Potassium: 11mg Sugar: 6g (7%)


## Ingredients

-   1/2 cup mayonnaise
-   1/4 cup honey
-   2 tablespoons yellow mustard
-   2 tablespoons ketchup
-   1 tablespoon lemon juice
-   1 teaspoon brown sugar
-   1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar

## Directions

1.  Combine all the ingredients in a small bowl and whisk to combine.
2.  Store in the refrigerator in an airtight container for up to 4 weeks
